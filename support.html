Support

Have a question about intavio or run into an issue? We are happy to help.

Just send us an email at:

support@intavio.app

What we can help with

This includes timer issues, general app questions, feature feedback, or anything that is not working as expected on your device.

For a faster reply

It helps if you include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened and how to reproduce it.
